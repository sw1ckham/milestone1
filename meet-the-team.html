Meet the Team

WHO ARE WE?

A family run business serving the South coast in Hampshire, we pride ourselves in combining honest workmanship and a friendly, trustworthy personal service that is not generally found nowadays

THE TOTAL SOLUTION

We have combined our skills in engineering, electrics and building to offer these all round quality units which has become our passion. A full home garden building installation includes solid foundations, full electrics, heating, full insulation and internal seamless plaster boarding, internal and external paint finishing, double glazing, quality flooring options and more!

BUILT TO LAST

We use individually nailed Shingles as our standard roofing material. They have a life span of at least 30 years. Superior 44mm finish interlocking logs emphasise the structural integrity making it extremely stable – this model is built to last!

BESPOKE TO YOU

Our experienced team of craftsmen are on hand and willing to help you create an insulated log cabin which is entirely unique to you and the perfect fit to your garden. The bespoke log cabin you want may simply be a small tweak based on an existing insulated garden building in our large range or come from a completely new design devised by you.

STRESS FREE

We are friendly, knowledgeable and efficient, and you will always be kept informed of key dates and other important information to ensure a smooth installation and stress free experience with minimum disruption.
